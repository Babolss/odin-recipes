Perfect Summer Fruit Salad

You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below,
but let's go over the basics:

Ingredients

For the sauce

  • fresh orange and lemon juices
  • brown sugar
  • grated orange and lemon zests
  • vanilla extract

For the fruit you'll need

  • pineapple
  • strawberries
  • kiwis
  • bananas
  • oranges
  • grapes
  • blueberries

Step by Step

  1. Make the sauce on the stove and let it cool.
  2. Arrange the fruits in a container, then pour the sauce over them.
  3. Cover and refrigerate to allow the flavors to meld.
